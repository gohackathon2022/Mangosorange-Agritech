JAN 2023

Polyhouse Projects

A polyhouse is a type of greenhouse that is typically made from polyethylene plastic film. They are used in agriculture to protect crops from extreme weather conditions and to extend the growing season. Inside the polyhouse, the temperature, humidity, and light levels can be controlled to optimize growth conditions for the plants. This can lead to higher yields and better quality crops.

Hi-Tech Polyhouse Projects

Hi-tech Polyhouses are advanced versions of traditional polyhouses that incorporate a wide range of technologies to optimize crop growth and productivity. It can also be equipped with advanced lighting systems, such as LED lights, which can be used to control the photoperiod of the plants, promoting growth and increasing yields.

NOV 2022

Hydroponic Projects

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil. Instead, plants are grown in a nutrient-rich solution, often water, which is delivered directly to the roots. The plants are usually supported by an inert medium such as gravel, rockwool, or coconut coir. This method of growing plants is often used in greenhouses, polyhouses, and indoor facilities, such as warehouses, and can offer many advantages over traditional soil-based farming.

Hi-Tech Hydroponic Projects

Hi-tech hydroponics in agriculture involves the use of advanced technology and techniques to optimize crop growth and productivity in hydroponic systems. This can also make use of precision agriculture techniques such as precision irrigation, nutrient management and crop monitoring. This can greatly improve crop yields, reduce labor costs and make the overall farming operation more efficient.

Aeroponic Projects

Aeroponics is a method of growing plants without soil, and it uses a mist or fog of nutrient-rich solution to provide plants with the nutrients they need to grow. This mist is delivered directly to the roots of the plants through a system of nozzles or misters. The plants are typically supported by a structure such as a mesh or slotted board, and their roots are suspended in the air.

Organic Farms

An organic farm is a type of farm that uses methods of production that do not involve the use of synthetic chemicals or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Organic farming is based on principles of sustainability and aims to create a closed-loop system in which waste is minimized, and natural resources are conserved.
