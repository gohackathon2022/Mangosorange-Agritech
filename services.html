Bringing Growth in Agriculture

MangosOrange Agritech Services can help farmers increase their efficiency, productivity, and sustainability, while addressing the challenges of food security, climate change, and environmental degradation. They also support the long-term viability of the agriculture industry and contribute to economic growth in rural areas. Agriculture services refer to a wide range of services that are provided to farmers, ranchers, and other members of the agriculture industry, with a focus on innovation and sustainability.

Services

What We Do

Research & Development

Developing new methods for growing crops in challenging environments such as drought or extreme temperatures.

Events

Events are held throughout the year and provide an opportunity for farmers to showcase their products and learn about new technologies and techniques in agriculture.

Projects

Agriculture projects can also focus on education and outreach, such as teaching people about the importance of agriculture or promoting sustainable farming practices.

MangosOrange Agritech services can help farmers increase their efficiency, productivity, and sustainability, while addressing the challenges of food security, climate change, and environmental degradation. These services can include:


  • FARM MANAGEMENT

    • Services such as crop planning, soil testing, and pest management that help farmers improve their operations and increase their productivity.

  • DIGITAL FARMING

    • Services that use digital technologies such as drones, sensors, and big data analytics to help farmers monitor and manage their operations, improve crop yields, and reduce input costs.

  • DATA ANALYSIS AND MODELING

    • Services that help farmers to analyze and model large datasets, such as weather, soil, or crop data to make more informed decisions about their operations.

  • LIVESTOCK MANAGEMENT

    • Services such as veterinary care, feeding, and breeding that help farmers improve the health and productivity of their livestock.

  • SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE

    • Services that help farmers adopt sustainable and regenerative farming methods, such as organic farming, regenerative agriculture, and other alternative farming methods.

  • CLIMATE-SMART AGRICULTURE

    • Services that help farmers adapt to and mitigate the effects of climate change on agriculture, such as precision irrigation and precision planting.
