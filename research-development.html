Research and Development



High-Tech and Sustainable Agriculture Techniques

MangosOrange Agritech Promote Modern Innovative Farming through research in technology, environmental conditions, soil, crops, seeds, pesticides, bio fertilizers, plant nutrition, automation, irrigation system, pest control and various other domains. Help publish journals both international & national on Agri-Tech and similar domains. Help promote and publish research papers in high-tech and modern farming using innovative ideas and research.

Objective of Program, Role and Responsibilities


  • PRODUCTIVITY AND EFFICIENCY

    • R&D in agriculture can help farmers increase yields, reduce costs, and improve the overall efficiency of crop and livestock production. This can help farmers increase their profitability and contribute to food security.

  • CROP BREEDING

    • Researchers develop new crop varieties that are more resistant to pests and diseases, have higher yields, and are better adapted to changing climate conditions.

  • FOOD SECURITY

    • Aims to develop sustainable and efficient ways of producing more food to feed a growing population, especially in the face of climate change, land degradation, and water scarcity.

  • SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE

    • R&D in agriculture can help develop new farming methods and technologies that are more sustainable and environmentally friendly, such as precision agriculture, organic farming, regenerative agriculture.

  • LIVESTOCK BREEDING

    • The development of new crop and livestock varieties that are more resistant to pests and diseases, have higher yields, and are better adapted to changing climate conditions.

  • BIOTECHNOLOGY

    • R&D in biotechnology can lead to the development of new solutions to improve crop yields, reduce the use of chemicals, and improve the quality of food.

Agriculture That Works for Future

MangosOrange Agritech Research and development in agriculture encompasses a wide range of activities aimed at improving crop yields, reducing pest and disease pressure, and increasing efficiency in farming operations. This can include work on breeding new crop varieties that are more resistant to disease or pests, developing new technologies for precision agriculture, and researching ways to optimize irrigation and fertilizer use.

Bringing Growth in Agriculture

The goal of MangosOrange Agritech Research and Development is to improve crop yields, reduce the use of chemical inputs and increase the efficiency of farming operations while maintaining the ecological balance.
