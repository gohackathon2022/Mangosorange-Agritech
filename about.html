About Us

MangosOrange Agritech India Private Limited is an Agritech startup envisaging the vision of creating startup culture in Agriculture using technology and innovation.

Research and Development – Promote Modern Innovative Farming through research in technology, environmental conditions, soil, crops, seeds, pesticides, bio fertilizers, plant nutrition, automation, irrigation system, pest control and various other domains. Help publish journals both international & national on Agri-Tech and similar domains. Help promote and publish research papers in high-tech and modern farming using innovative ideas and research. This can include work on breeding new crop varieties that are more resistant to disease or pests, developing new technologies for precision agriculture, and researching ways to optimize irrigation and fertilizer use.

Modern Innovative Farming – Consulting and implementing the research and innovation through tie up with universities, colleges, institutes, government bodies, international research organisations, international companies and other agencies of the same or allied domain. Help develop entrepreneurship through creating centre of excellence, innovation labs, training labs, high-tech farms. Seek funding from investors and provide implementation of high-tech farms and build commercial farming setup for interested educational institutions, private bodies and individuals, farmers and investors.

Educating & Consulting – MangosOrange Agritech India lays a very strong emphasis on world Class Training for Research scholars, faculties, students, entrepreneurs, Farmers and other groups in various new technologies, equipment, govt schemes, etc. We look forward to tie up with universities to embed our research methodologies in their curriculum, carry out joint research, launch various degree, diploma and certificate programs in collaboration with universities, training institutes, coaching centres and other such organisations. conduct series of seminars, workshops, hackathons, ideathons, events related to education and training of farming and agricultural innovations in all allied domains.

MangosOrange Agritech India Private Limited is an Agritech startup envisaging the vision of creating startup culture in Agriculture using technology and innovation.
